# Municipal Knowledge Hub Question Bank

## Question 1

Question:
What does IDP stand for?

Options:
A. Internal Development Process
B. Infrastructure Delivery Plan
C. Integrated Development Plan
D. Integrated Delivery Process

Answer:
C

Explanation:
IDP stands for Integrated Development Plan.


---

## Question 2

Question:
Which sphere of government is responsible for municipalities?

Options:
A. Local Government
B. Provincial Government
C. National Government
D. Judicial Government

Answer:
A

Explanation:
Municipalities form part of the local sphere of government.

## Question 3

Question:
What is the purpose of a ward committee?

Options:
A. To collect taxes
B. To support community participation
C. To approve budgets
D. To manage municipal staff

Answer:
B

Explanation:
Ward committees help communities participate in local government.
